      Title: Cookies Test
 Categories: Snacks, Cookies & Squares
   Servings: 2

------------------------------Dry Ingredients-------------------------------
      1    c. brown sugar
    3/4    c. granulated sugar
      2    c. all-purpose flour
      1    tsp. baking soda
------------------------------Fat and Liquids-------------------------------
      2    eggs
      1    c. peanut butter
      1    tsp. vanilla extract
      1    c. shortening; at room tempe
