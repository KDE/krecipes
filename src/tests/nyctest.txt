Snacks,	Cookies & Squares

3/4 c. granulated sugar
1 c. brown sugar
2 c. all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 c. shortening -- softened,at room temperature	
1 c. peanut butter
2 whole eggs
1 tsp. vanilla extract

Drop by spoonful on greased cookie sheet.  Bake in moderate oven.
